Privacy Policy

Especially for Singles is committed to protecting your privacy.  We encourage a careful reading of this document to understand the information we collect about you, how that information is used, how your information is shared with others, and your rights regarding your information.  We encourage you to check back for any possible updates to this policy in the future, as it may change at any time.

What we collect and why

To allow us to help you find and make meaningful connections with other users, we need to collect certain basic information to help build a profile that is presented to possible connections.  We also collect usage and error logs to help us improve the experience.

Information you provide to us

  • As part of the required profile creation step of using our services, you choose to provide at least your first name, email address, birthday, gender, and one photo of yourself.  You also provide basic information about the ages and locations of the profiles of other users you are interested in.
  • You can optionally provide other information to assist in making connections with other users, as well as additional photos of yourself to help your profile stand out.
  • Any information provided when taking part in optional surveys.
  • Any information provided to support@especiallyforsingles.com .
  • Any information you choose to provide when interacting with other users via chat or otherwise.

Information we collect

  • We periodically collect precise geolocation information about you, to determine how or if your profile is shown to other users based on their geolocation information or preferences.  Your permission to do so is required to use our services.
  • We periodically collect usage information such as when you last accessed our services, the parts of our services you have interacted with, whether certain information has been presented to you, and the interactions between you and other users including chats, likes or dislikes, and the times that such interactions took place.
  • We may collect information from the device you use to access our services.  We may collect IP addresses, hardware IDs, browser or language preferences, advertising IDs, or other device identifiers in order to assist in the operation of our services.
  • We may use cookies or pixel tags (e.g. web beacons, pixels, SDKs) on web pages, ads, and emails. These measures may be used to gather information about the usage rates of various aspects of our service.  Advertising companies may also use these measures to collect information about the viewing of or any interactions with their advertisements.

Information we receive from others

  • Other users may provide information about you as they interact with your profile, report or block your profile, or chat with you.
  • You may elect to share social media profile information with us when creating or logging into your account.
  • We may receive information about you from advertising partners where Especially for Singles ads are displayed to you on a partner service.

How we use your information

  • In the process of signing up and using our services, you agree to have your profile shown to other users.  They will have the ability to interact with your profile, liking, disliking, reporting, or blocking it.  If you have indicated that you like another user profile, they may send you chat messages.
  • Your information may be provided to third parties for data hosting, maintenance, analytics, marketing, and payment processing. Your information may also be provided to advertising partners in an anonymized form to assist with displaying targeted advertisements.
  • We may share your information with law enforcement in order to comply with court orders or to conform to other applicable law.
  • We may share your information with third parties to help defend against legal actions taken against us.
  • We may combine non-personal information we collect with additional non-personal information collected from other sources. We also may share aggregated, non-personal information, or personal information in hashed, non-human readable form, with third parties, including advertisers and investors, for the purpose of conducting business analysis or other business purposes.

Third party services

  • In the course of normal operation of our services, you may be presented with advertisements or links sent via chat from other users.  Clicking on these links may direct you to third party websites or services that are not subject to this policy.  

Accessing your information

  • You can edit your information in the profile section of our services, you may also permanently remove your information in the same section.  We may retain information about you if you are found to be in violation of our terms of service to aid in preventing you from creating additional accounts or otherwise attempting to circumvent our policies.  We may retain certain information about you for a period of three months in the event that it may be necessary to investigate unlawful or harmful conduct.

Children’s Privacy

  • We restrict usage of our services to members over the age of 18 only.  We do not knowingly collect or maintain information about members under this age.  If you suspect a profile on our services is being utilized by an underage person, please use the reporting mechanism available on our service, or reach out to support@especiallyforsingles.com

Location of site and data

  • Our services are operated in the United States. If you are located in the European Union or elsewhere outside of the United States, please be aware that any information you provide to us will be transferred to and stored in the United States. By using our services and/or providing us with your information, you consent to this transfer.

How to contact us

  • For any questions about this privacy policy, please contact support@especiallyforsingles.com
